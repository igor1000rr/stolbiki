## Full Description
Snatch Highrise is an original strategy board game for 2 players powered by AlphaZero-style AI trained on 10M+ self-play games.

🎯 SIMPLE TO LEARN, DEEP TO MASTER
10 stands, 11 chips each. Place your chips, transfer between stands, and close them to claim ownership. First to close 6 stands wins! The golden ★ stand breaks 5:5 ties.

🤖 ALPHAZERO AI
Play against an AI with policy + value neural network (859K parameters). 5 difficulty levels from Easy to Impossible. Uses Monte Carlo Tree Search with learned move priors — like DeepMind's AlphaZero.

🌐 ONLINE MULTIPLAYER
Create a room, share the code, and play with friends. Tournament mode, spectator mode, in-game chat, and rematch.

🧩 200+ PUZZLES
Daily and weekly challenges. Puzzle bank with easy, medium, and hard levels. Leaderboards.

🏆 PROFILE & ACHIEVEMENTS
Track your rating, win streaks, and stats. Unlock 33 achievements.

⚡ FEATURES
• Haptic feedback for every action
• Offline play — AI works without internet
• Hint system and position trainer
• AI game review — every move rated like chess.com
• 11 customizable themes, chip & stand skins
• Swap rule for balanced gameplay
• Game replay viewer
• Print & Play PDF

🔒 No ads, no trackers, no data selling.
